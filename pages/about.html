About Me And My Project

About Me

Hi, I'm Halil Furkan Türkmen, an aspiring web developer and lifelong sci-fi enthusiast. I created Project Callisto to deepen my web development skills and to build a dedicated space for Stargate fans to explore this beloved franchise.

This journey has been challenging but incredibly rewarding. Stargate isn't just a series of movies and shows to me — it's a passion that started in my teenage years and continues to inspire me today.

I'm grateful to everyone who has contributed to the Stargate universe, and to those who share the love and knowledge of this amazing world. Thank you for visiting Project Callisto.

About Project Callisto

Project Callisto is a fan-made archive dedicated to the Stargate universe. It offers detailed information about the shows, characters, and lore, designed to be a resource for both longtime fans and newcomers.
